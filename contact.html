Common questions

Before you write in.

How do I schedule a pickup?

Use the form above or call your local hub directly — most pickups can be scheduled for the next business day.

Can I redirect a parcel mid‑transit?

Yes, up until it reaches the final sorting hub. Enter your tracking number on the homepage to request a redirect.

Do you offer same‑day delivery?

Same‑day is available within select metro hubs for parcels booked before 11:00 AM local time.

How is freight pricing calculated?

Freight is quoted per route based on weight, volume, and pickup frequency — request a quote and a routing specialist will follow up.
